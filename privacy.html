隐私政策

生效日期：2026 年 7 月 11 日 ｜ 最近更新：2026 年 7 月 13 日

Wenbo Qiu（以下简称"我们"）非常重视您的个人信息与隐私保护。本《隐私政策》旨在向您说明，当您使用 Bolt SSH（以下简称"本应用"）时，我们如何收集、使用与保护您的相关信息，以及您所享有的权利。

本应用以"按需授权、最小收集"为原则。您的 SSH 凭据、私钥及终端会话内容始终保留在您的设备上或直接传输至您自己的服务器，我们的服务器不会接触这些数据。

一、我们如何收集与使用您的信息

场景收集的信息用途存储位置
主机管理 主机 IP、端口、用户名、标签名称、标记颜色（不含密码，除非您主动开启"保存密码"） 在设备本地保存主机列表 设备本地（SharedPreferences）
SSH 密钥 您导入的私钥文件内容 用于 SSH 密钥认证 设备系统 Keychain（加密存储）
保存的密码（可选） 您主动开启"保存密码"时的登录密码 免去每次连接时手动输入 设备系统 Keychain（加密存储）
应用锁 应用锁开关状态 下次启动时决定是否需要 Face ID / 指纹验证 设备本地
匿名使用统计 应用内行为事件（如：打开应用、连接尝试成功/失败、新建/关闭标签、导入/删除密钥等），不含任何可识别您身份的信息 了解功能使用情况，指导产品改进 我们自托管的 Pulse 统计服务（pulse.wondergarden.top）

二、SSH 连接与终端内容

您在终端中输入的命令及服务器的响应内容直接在您的设备与您自己的 SSH 服务器之间传输，采用 SSH 协议加密，不经过我们的任何服务器，我们无法访问这些内容。

主机密钥（Host Key）的哈希值存储在设备本地，用于连接时的 TOFU（首次信任）验证，防止中间人攻击，同样不上传至我们的服务器。

四、设备权限说明

  • 网络：用于建立 SSH 连接，直接连接您指定的服务器，不经过我们的代理。
  • Face ID / 指纹（生物识别）：仅在您开启应用锁时使用，认证在设备本地完成，生物特征数据不离开设备。
  • 文件访问：仅在您主动导入 SSH 私钥文件时使用，读取后即存入 Keychain，不保留原始文件副本。

五、信息的存储与安全

  • SSH 私钥与密码存储于系统 Keychain，受设备级加密保护。
  • 匿名统计数据存储于我们自托管的服务器（中国境内）。
  • 我们不出售、不租用您的任何信息。

六、您的权利

  • 查看与删除：您可在应用内随时查看、编辑或删除已保存的主机和密钥；
  • 关闭统计：如不希望发送匿名统计，可通过系统设置关闭应用的网络权限（注意这会同时影响 SSH 连接）；
  • 投诉与反馈：如有疑问，可通过下方"联系我们"与我们联系。

七、未成年人保护

本应用主要面向成年专业用户。我们不会主动向未成年人收集个人信息。

八、政策更新

我们可能适时修订本政策并更新"生效日期"。对于重大变更，我们会通过应用内提示告知。继续使用即视为接受修订后的政策。

Privacy Policy

Effective: July 11, 2026 ｜ Last updated: July 13, 2026

Wenbo Qiu ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use Bolt SSH (the "App"), and the rights you have over that information.

We collect data on a "need-based, minimal" principle. Your SSH credentials, private keys, and terminal session content always stay on your device or travel directly to your own server — our servers never touch this data.

1. Information We Collect and How We Use It

ScenarioInformationPurposeStorage
Host management Host IP, port, username, label, color tag (passwords excluded unless you enable "Save password") Store host list locally on device On-device (SharedPreferences)
SSH keys Private key file content you import SSH key authentication System Keychain (encrypted)
Saved password (optional) Login password, when you explicitly enable "Save password" Skip manual password entry on connect System Keychain (encrypted)
App Lock App lock on/off state Determine whether Face ID / fingerprint is required at next launch On-device
Anonymous usage analytics In-app behavior events (e.g. app open, connection success/failure, tab and key management actions) — no personally identifiable information Understand feature usage to guide product improvements Our self-hosted Pulse analytics (pulse.wondergarden.top)

2. SSH Connections & Terminal Content

Commands you type and server responses travel directly between your device and your own SSH server, encrypted by the SSH protocol. They do not pass through our servers and we have no access to them.

Host key fingerprints are stored locally on your device for TOFU (Trust On First Use) verification, protecting against man-in-the-middle attacks. They are never uploaded to our servers.

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party advertising or cross-app tracking SDK, and does not collect device identifiers for ad targeting.

4. Device Permissions

  • Network: used to establish SSH connections directly to the server you specify — not proxied through us.
  • Face ID / fingerprint (biometrics): used only when App Lock is enabled; authentication is performed on-device and biometric data never leaves your device.
  • File access: used only when you actively import an SSH private key file; after import the key is stored in the Keychain and no copy of the original file is retained.

5. Storage & Security

  • SSH private keys and passwords are stored in the system Keychain, protected by device-level encryption.
  • Anonymous analytics data is stored on our self-hosted server (located in mainland China).
  • We do not sell or rent any of your information.

6. Your Rights

  • Access & deletion: you can view, edit, or delete saved hosts and keys in the App at any time;
  • Opt out of analytics: you can revoke the App's network permission in system settings (note this will also disable SSH connections);
  • Feedback & complaints: contact us via the address below with any questions.

7. Children

The App is intended primarily for adult professional users. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may revise this Policy from time to time and update the "Effective" date. For material changes we will notify you via an in-app notice. Continued use after a change takes effect constitutes acceptance.
